FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a permit for a fence in Sunnyvale?

Most fences under 7 feet don't require a building permit, but corner visibility and setback rules still apply. We verify requirements for your lot before starting.

How long does installation take?

A typical backyard fence (100–150 linear feet) takes 1–2 days. Larger projects or custom gates may extend to 3–4 days.

What areas do you serve?

Sunnyvale, Mountain View, Santa Clara, Cupertino, Los Altos, Campbell, and surrounding South Bay communities.

What warranty do you provide?

All installations include a one-year workmanship warranty. Material warranties vary by manufacturer and are passed through in full.
